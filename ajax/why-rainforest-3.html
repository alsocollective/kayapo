Why Rainforests Matter?

Once lost, it is gone forever.
Help stabilize the world´s climate by storing carbon, mitigating climate change, regulating rainfall and cycling pure water and air.
The forest produces half of its own rainfall and much of the rain south of the Amazon and east of the Andes.
Rainforests provide resources for people including food, medicine and recreation. Did you know that a huge percentage of the world´s diet originated in the rainforest?
Home to indigenous people and their unique cultures that live in harmony with the natural world.
