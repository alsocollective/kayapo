100% of funds donated will go directly to Kayapo rainforest preservation projects.

$20

Will fund the protection activities of 1000 acres of Kayapo lands for 1 year.

$50

Buys 3 Kayapo families supplies (machetes and sacs) to collect Brazil nuts for trade.

$100

Buys 70 liters of gas for use on patrol of the nearly 2500km of territorial boundaries.

$500

Pays for one half hour of overflight border surveillance.

$1000

Pays for eight weeks of 4X4 vehicle fuel and maintenance. Vehicles are used for border patrol and also to support Kayapo marketing of their sustainably harvested non-timber forest products.

$10,000

Pays one year salary for a Kayapo to stay in town and work at their own organization to learn administrative skills and gain greater understanding of outside society. Kayapo who work at their local organizations help to interpret and explain issues to their communities.
